MSHS Dinner for a Cause

The Minglanilla Science High School will be holding a Dinner for a Cause this Saturday, March 25, 2017 as a fund-raising program to support the school’s expenses in the upcoming Sugat Kabanhawan Festival 2017. It will be an evening filled with laughter and excitement as parents, students, teachers, and alumni witness the talent and magnificence of MingScians.

The main highlight of this event will be the HaraRajah 2017, where candidates take you out of this world in a cosmic adventure as they represent the celestial bodies and quintessential elements. Prepare to be stunned in awe as you witness the candidates’ overflowing beauty, confidence, and wit that is the brand of the school.

MSHS celebrates 20th Foundation Year

The Minglanilla Science High School is now on its 20th Year Foundation Anniversary. The school was founded in 1996, with Carmencita Denampo as supervisor for the high school and elementary department, and was named the Minglanilla Science and Technology School, which is shortened to SciTech. People still use this moniker in reference to the school.

The celebration of the 20th foundation of the school is one of the highlights to the upcoming MSHS Dinner for a Cause. In its 20th year, the school emphasizes the changes that occurred in the campus over the time, leading towards a modern and innovative approaches in education.

MSHS Attends first ever Spark Summit

Selected students of Minglanilla Science High School attended the first ever Spark Summit held on March 11,2017 at the SM Seaside City, Cebu. The event was hosted by the Southwestern University to promote and spark the Leader, Storyteller, Innovator, and Artist in each of the students who attended the summit. The summit features forums on timely topics that hope to spark interest in and provoke conversations on leadership, innovation, storytelling, and design. There were four talks held during the course of the summit: Spark the Leader, Spark the Innovator, Spark the Storyteller, and Spark the Artist, where students are distributed to the different talks according to their natural affinity.

Ongoing Preparation for the Moving Up Ceremony 2017

As of March 24, 2017, the Grade 10 Batch Syntixi of the Minglanilla Science High School are currently doing some preparations for the upcoming Completion Ceremony this April 7,2017. Every day, the batch practices their batch song, completion song, and gratitude songs for parents and teachers, entitled "This is our Goodbye","Syntixi Are We?", "Ma,Pa, Salamat", and "Salamat, Thank You, Salamat" (See Lyrics), respectively which were composed and written by their fellow batch mates Sierlou Hidalgo, Sierlae Hidalgo, Radelle Mendaros, Jelliane Canonigo, and Joshua Montefalcon. Before they start their practice, selected students from their batch are tasked to relay their Prophecy, their Happiest Moments during their 4 years in MSHS, and some Insights and Learnings about their experiences.

Keep posted for the latest happenings as the date of the Batch Syntixi Completion Ceremony is nearing...
